Counsel

David Borins

David has been practicing labour and employment law for over 25 years. He has appeared before BC Supreme Court, Federal Court Trial Division, the Federal Court of Appeal, federal and provincial labour relations boards and human rights tribunals, WorkSafe BC, and other administrative tribunals. He represents trade unions in grievance arbitrations and mediations, bargaining unit certifications, and governance. David also acts for employers and employees in non-union workplace related matters and is available to assist with workplace investigations. He has extensive experience in the area of societies and professional associations, providing representation in complex litigation matters, and can provide advice in connection with governance-related issues and member discipline and disputes.

Practice Areas

Labour & Employment

We assist trade unions with a wide range of matters including grievance arbitrations, federal and provincial labour relations board matters, and other complex labour relations issues. We also assist employees and employers on matters such as employment contracts, terminations, constructive dismissals, and disciplinary issues.

→

Societies & Non-Profits

We provide full service to societies and other membership-based organizations and can assist with matters such as governance, bylaw review, elections, internal disputes, labour matters, and other issues faced by non-profit organizations. We can provide the services of an experienced parliamentarian to chair or assist with meetings of any size.

→

Statutory Tribunals

We provide advice and representation in matters before administrative tribunals such as the WorkSafeBC, the Workers' Compensation Appeal Tribunal, BC Human Rights Tribunal, and Hospital Appeal Board. We can assist with appeals and judicial reviews of tribunal decisions under both provincial and federal jurisdiction.

→

Workplace Investigations

We conduct harassment and other workplace investigations for unions and employers. We represent employees in workplace investigations.

→

Government Relations

We advise and represent our clients before all levels of government on public policy matters including regulatory and statutory amendments.

→

Litigation

We represent our clients before all levels of court on cases requiring a tailored strategy, including labour and employment matters, societies and non-profit matters, judicial reviews, and Charter issues.
